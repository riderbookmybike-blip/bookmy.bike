GOT QUESTIONS?
What is the HF Deluxe on road price in India?
The HF Deluxe on road price is competitive but depends on the city, RTO, and insurance charges. For the most accurate price, visit your nearest Hero dealership or check online.
What colour options are available in HF Deluxe colour variants?
The HF Deluxe colour choices include Sports Red Black, Black Nexus Blue, Candy Blazing Red, and more. Each shade complements the bike’s classic styling while adding a modern, vibrant touch.
What are the highlights of HF Deluxe specification?
The HF Deluxe specification set features xSENS FI Technology, i3S, engine cut-off at fall, all-weather easy start, 130mm rear brake, and a side stand engine cut-off. It’s built for smart performance, safety, and fuel efficiency.
How much mileage does the HF Deluxe mileage offer in daily use?
The HF Deluxe mileage is among the best in its class, offering an impressive number under standard riding conditions. Its fuel-efficient engine combined with i3S makes it an economical choice for everyday riders.
Is the HF Deluxe on road price justified by its features?
Absolutely! The HF Deluxe on road price brings great value with smart features, top-notch mileage, and Hero’s trusted reliability. It’s one of the most cost-effective commuter bikes designed for long-term daily use.
