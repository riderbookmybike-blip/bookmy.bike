FASCINO 125 Fi-Hybrid FAQs

FAQs

The Yamaha Fascino 125 Fi Hybrid comes in Stylish & Exciting color variants such as Metallic Black, Cool Blue Metallic, and Dark Matte Blue, available in both Drum and Disc variants. Moreover, the Disc variants have an additional selection of colors, namely Cyan Blue, Metallic Light Green, Vivid Red Spl, Matte Black Spl, Dark Matte Blue Spl and Matte Grey Spl.

The new Yamaha Fascino Fi Hybrid is powered by a BS-VI compliant, air-cooled, fuel injected (Fi), 125 cc blue core hybrid engine.

The Yamaha Fascino Fi Hybrid does come equipped with Y-Connect Bluetooth app featuring, trip distance, battery voltage, average speed, Last parked location and Malfunction Notification functions. Check out the details of how to connect Bluetooth. (https://www.yamaha-motor-india.com/bluetooth-connect.html)

The Yamaha Fascino Fi Hybrid has a fuel tank capacity of 5.2 liters.

The price of Yamaha Fascino Fi Hybrid starts at for Drum Variant (Ex-Showroom Price in Delhi) and for Disc Variant (Ex-Showroom Price in Delhi).

*Price may change without prior notice
